This content is injected with a WinJS HTMLControl.

Fragment got custom functions, properties and behavior from WinJS Contrib.

You will have shorthand to scoped selector like the q, or qAll functions. You will also be able to add a "data-page-member" attribute on DOM node to map those elements on your control instance. If this element is a control, you get the control mapped on your page instead of the element.

Another custom attributes, "data-page-action", and "data-page-link" make elements interactive and for "data-page-action", map it to a function on your page, like this

You will also manage events and databindings with the EventTracker (injected in a property called eventTracker), and much more.

The fragment mixin also expand page/fragment with custom steps that you could benefit from.
